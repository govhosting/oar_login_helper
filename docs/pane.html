Organisations and Appointments Register (OAR) is restricted to authorised users only

By accepting this message, you are acknowledging:

  1. that you will or have read, understood and agree to comply with the OAR Terms and Conditions; and
  2. user activity is monitored and audited.
The OAR Terms and Conditions are available from the links list on the left side of the OAR home screen. If you are logging in for the first time you must immediately read, understand and agree to comply with the OAR Terms and Conditions, indicated by your continued access to OAR.

Issues logging in?

If you have trouble logging in for the first time, it may not be as simple as your username and password being incorrect. There may be a number of reasons for this. To determine the cause of the issue, refer to the 'OAR - Log in issue and resolution guide' that was sent to you when your account was created. If you cannot find the resolution guide, please email directory.support@finance.gov.au with a description of the issue.
